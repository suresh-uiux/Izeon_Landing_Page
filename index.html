About the Hiring Week

Exclusive Product-Based Company Hiring Week is a focused hiring initiative by Izeon IT Training, designed exclusively for product companies, funded startups, and R&D centers seeking deployment-ready engineering talent.

This is not a mass placement drive. It is a curated hiring program where only pre-assessed, project-trained candidates are presented to hiring teams, significantly reducing screening time and onboarding effort.

  • Curated Talent Pool:Pre-screened candidates with proven technical skills
  • Industry-Ready:Trained on latest technologies and best practices
  • Deployment-Ready: Candidates ready to make immediate impact

Candidate Readiness Model

Our three-tier readiness model ensures you get access to candidates who are truly prepared to excel in your organization.

Pre-Assessed

All candidates undergo rigorous technical assessments covering coding skills, problem-solving abilities, and domain knowledge.

  • Technical screening completed
  • Coding & problem-solving evaluated
  • Soft skills & communication assessed

Industry-Trained

Comprehensive training programs aligned with industry standards, covering modern technologies and best practices.

  • Product-oriented curriculum
  • Real-time project exposure
  • Agile & team-based development practices

Deployment-Ready

Candidates are prepared to contribute immediately with minimal onboarding, ready to tackle real-world challenges.

  • Familiar with SDLC & Git workflows
  • Exposure to production-level scenarios
  • Minimal onboarding required
